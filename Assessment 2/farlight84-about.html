About Farlight 84

Farlight Games, headquartered in Singapore, is the global publishing brand of Lilith Games, the creator of renowned titles such as Farlight 84 and many others. Our establishment has marked a significant leap forward in our mission to deliver locally resonant experiences to our players worldwide. We strive to offer the best games to our global community. In Farlight 84, you get to play as heroes with unique skills to outsmart your enemies, unleashing special abilities to catch them off guard... AND you get to collect adorable Buddies to join you as loyal companions in battle. Buddy up, it’s time to Battle Royale! Buddy Orb - Catch Anything Just aim and throw--Buddy Orb lets you store vehicles, bunkers, and adorable Buddies, making them ready to deploy at critical moments. Bunkers - Portable Tactics Deploy them anytime by storing these bunkers in a Buddy Orb. Then choose the ideal weapon to overcome terrain disadvantages. Don't worry about being caught out in the open anymore. Buddies - Summon to Battle Buddies are more than just adorable—they're powerful assets in battle. Perform rescues from afar, redefine safe zones, or blast yourself away from danger... Anything is possible! Heroes - Roles to Play Farlight 84 features four distinct roles and dozens of cool-looking heroes. Select one to complement your squad and strategize—it's okay to not aim well, use your skills! Jetslide - Show Your Move Eager to flaunt your movement skills in Battle Royale? Here's your chance. Jetslide your way down the slope to confuse your enemies. Defeat them before they even know you're there. Skill Tree - Level...UP! A unified level-up experience. Fight to level up, and assign skill points to activate unique skill effects as you advance within a match. Feel your growth in every single game! Weapons - Loot & Upgrade Shotguns, sniper rifles, assault rifles, SMGs... With realistic 3D models and a wide range of firearms, you're in for an intense shooter experience. Also: hidden weapons waiting to be discovered in Airdrops! One Account. Cross-platforms Farlight 84 is fully cross-compatible. Rally your friends for cross-platform action anytime, anywhere, and watch your friendship levels skyrocket!
