Game Modes

Experience intense battles across a variety of gameplay modes.

Battle Royale

Each team drops in from pods from a predetermined path that they can deviate from if they so choose. Starting with no gear, they land and loot every weapon, item, and anything else that they need to survive. They need to battle all other teams and secure the top spot to win. At the start of the match, each player has a chance to respawn, and after a certain period of time, all remaining respawns are disabled. At a certain interval, Air drops are delivered from Isle City to aid the capsulers in the battlefield.
