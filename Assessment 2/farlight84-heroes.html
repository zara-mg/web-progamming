Meet Our Heroes

Maychelle

Reveals enemies with sound waves, deploys wall-piercing speakers, and summons a stage with a one-way barrier for safe ally fire.

Momoi

Transforms into a carryable dino, summons a 3-sided shield with her pet, and calls in a fighting dino companion.

Beau

Bombards two locations with airstrikes that ignore cover and gains increased strike power after using any skill.

Freddie

Deploys a swappable clone that reveals enemies on destruction, throws a confining orb trap, and summons a clone with a speed boost that auto-teleports him when downed.

Maggie

Dashes sideways with her jetpack, flies upward, and enters Overload mode to reduce cooldowns, extended by knockdowns.

Nightwatch

Deploys an auto-attacking Sheriff bot, sends a revealing drone that slows enemies, and enters Rampage Mode to enhance all drone skills and reduce cooldowns.
