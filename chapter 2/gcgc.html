Good Coffee, Great Coffee

Good Coffee, Great Coffee by TapBlaze is a fun and realistic coffee shop simulation game. You take on the role of a barista, crafting the perfect cup of coffee while managing customer expectations and running a successful café.

Gameplay

In this interactive simulation, you’ll prepare different types of coffee, handle customer requests, and make strategic upgrades to improve your café.

  • Make Coffee: Follow customer orders carefully and use the right ingredients to satisfy their cravings.
  • Manage Resources: Balance expenses and earnings to keep your café thriving.
  • Improve Your Shop: Upgrade equipment and enhance your menu to attract more customers.

Game Features

Realistic Coffee Experience: Brew espresso, cappuccinos, lattes, and more!

Engaging Customers: Serve a variety of customers, each with unique preferences.

Beautiful Graphics: Enjoy a charming, hand-drawn art style.

Challenging Orders: Test your memory and speed as the café gets busier.
